About Fanap Payment:

FANAP PAYMENT, a subsidiary of FANAP ICT holding, has been providing different customers with a wide range of payment solutions for more than a decade. Thanks to our young, talented, and highly motivated team, we are known as a leading company in Iran payment industry. Our purpose is to facilitate the purchase process both for merchants and consumers through delivering in-store, mobile and web-based services; merchants receive funds without any concerns or complexity and consumers benefit a fast, easy, and secure payment experience. We dedicate ourselves to enable a world-class seamless payment experience.
